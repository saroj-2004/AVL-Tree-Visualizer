About AVL Trees

An AVL tree is a self-balancing binary search tree where the heights of the two child subtrees of any node differ by at most one.

Rotations

  • LL Rotation: Right rotation when left subtree is heavy
  • RR Rotation: Left rotation when right subtree is heavy
  • LR Rotation: Left-Right rotation for left-right case
  • RL Rotation: Right-Left rotation for right-left case

Balance Factor

Balance Factor = Height(Left Subtree) - Height(Right Subtree)

Valid range: -1, 0, 1
